## Current and Upcoming Teaching

### IS220 — Introduction to Information Systems

**Kennesaw State University**  
**Fall 2026 (Planned)**

This course introduces fundamental concepts in information systems, including the role of technology in organizations, data management, and emerging digital platforms. The course emphasizes the practical application of information systems to support business processes and decision-making.
